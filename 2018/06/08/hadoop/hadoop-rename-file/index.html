Hadoop的API使用：文件重命名
2018-06-08|Hadoop
Word count: 343|Reading time: 1 min

问题背景

假设HDFS上日志文件的形式如下：

/logs/20171011/188.txt
/logs/20171011/2sf.txt
/logs/20171011/36t.txt
/logs/20171012/1ns.txt
/logs/20171012/2sfs.txt
/logs/20171012/37hjd.txt

现在要求，使用HDFS API实现日志文件的重命名，输出格式如下：

/logs/20171011/20171011-0.txt
/logs/20171011/20171011-1.txt
/logs/20171011/20171011-2.txt
/logs/20171012/20171012-0.txt
/logs/20171012/20171012-1.txt
/logs/20171012/20171012-2.txt

也就是实现日志文件命名的规则化。
